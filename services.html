My Services

Comprehensive Educational Support for Every Child's Unique Journey

Academic Services

  • Orton-Gillingham Reading Intervention - Evidence-based, multisensory approach
  • Multi-sensory Math Instruction - Hands-on learning for mathematical concepts
  • Writing Support - From basic skills to creative expression
  • Social and Emotional Support - Building confidence and self-esteem
  • Self-regulation Strategies - Managing stress and anxiety
  • Educational Assessments - Understanding your child's needs
  • Educational Consultation - IEP and ETR guidance

Service Delivery Options

In-Person

One-on-one or small group sessions in local libraries or community spaces

Virtual

Online sessions with interactive tools and resources

Blended

Combination of in-person and virtual instruction

Who We Serve

Ohio homeschooling families with children who have special needs including:

  • ADHD
  • Autism Spectrum Disorders
  • Dyslexia
  • Dyscalculia
  • Other Learning Differences
