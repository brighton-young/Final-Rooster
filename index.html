What is Special Education?

Personalized instruction designed to meet the unique needs of students with IEPs

Special education services provide individualized support for students with learning differences. As a Licensed Intervention Specialist, I work with students who have an Individualized Education Program (IEP) to help them reach their full potential in reading, writing, and math.

Whether your child is homeschooled or attends public/private school, I provide evidence-based interventions tailored to their specific learning needs, helping them build confidence and achieve academic success.

Another scholarship for students who have an IEP

Free Educational Support for Students on IEPs

Learning Aid Ohio provides funding for in-person, supplemental learning support for students with IEPs. Families are matched with tutors who help students stay on track with their unique academic goals—based on their individual needs and school curriculum.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Ohio residents
  • Students on an IEP
  • Household income under 400% of the Federal Poverty Level (e.g., $124,800 for a family of four)

How It Works:

  1. Apply online at learningaidohio.org
  2. Search for a tutor by expertise, grade, travel distance, and availability
  3. Schedule an introductory interview to ensure a great fit
